&\\
 &= \prod_{i} ( 1 - Pr(occupied | B_i ) ) \\
&\\
&= \prod_{i} ( 1 - w_iB_i )  \\
&\\
Pr(occupied) &= 1 - \prod_{i} ( 1 - w_iB_i ) \\
